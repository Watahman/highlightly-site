Core features

Everything you need to collect and organize online research.

01

Highlight and annotate

Select text on a webpage, highlight it in a color, and attach a note without leaving the page.

02

Keep the original source

Every saved highlight stays connected to the webpage it came from, so you can reopen the source later.

03

Search your library

Find saved highlights, edit notes, remove old items, or open the original page from one clear library.

04

Organize with projects

Group highlights from different websites into projects for school, work, planning, or research.

05

Create custom colors

Add your own named colors, such as “Quote”, “Important”, or “Follow up”, and reuse them anywhere.

06

Export to Markdown

Turn your saved material into portable Markdown for notes, documents, knowledge bases, or reports.
